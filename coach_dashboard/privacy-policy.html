terms & Conditions

The participant must follow the nutrition and or the workout program in order to achieve their desired result easily.

When subscribing to the nutritional or workout program or both (depending on the package), the participant will be allowed only start the program three days after the date of subscription and will not be allowed to choose any date before that. If the subscriber chooses to start the program after three days from the date of subscription, the program will be sent within 72 hours. If the participant chooses the date of program to start after 5 days or more from the date of subscription, the program will be sent within 48 hours before the starting date of the program which is chosen by the participant. When the participant chooses to start the program, they will have access to the program content, and they will not have access before that.

From the date you purchase the program, you are allowed to choose the starting date of the program up to one month only

When the subscriber purchases the nutrition or workout package, or both. The content will only be viewed ONLY on the user dashboard. After the starting time of the program, the subscriber will be allowed to view the content of the program according to the duration of the package, and the subscriber will be denied the access to view the content of the program at the end of the package duration (the duration of the package could be one month, two months, 3 months, etc.)

The participant must purchase Nutrition weight device

The participant must update their weight and body fat index weekly through the user dashboard (which is available after registration in the program). The weekly update starts from the first day on which the participant decides to start their nutrition and or workout program. If the weight and fat index are not updated on time, the supervision service will be terminated with the participant for not cooperating with the work team to achieve the participant goal

While the package is active and participant had a disease, encountered accident or any other serious problem, the validity of the package will be extended only when we receive an official medical certificate (please upload the medical certificate file through the user dashboard)

When the customer buys the package and transfer there will be no refund at all.

The nutrition and workout program must not be re-used after you under any circumstances of and must not be shared with others. We do not bear the slightest responsibility in the event of not benefiting from or causing damage to the body, as the body always requires new considerations.
